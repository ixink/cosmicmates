Welcome to the Cosmic Mate

The stars are open to everyone. Think beyond the space. Join us in exploring the vast universe, where every star may host new worlds. This platform provides interactive and engaging materials to learn about exoplanets, especially for those with limited access to traditional learning tools.

Our Mission

We aim to make the mysteries of exoplanets accessible to everyone. Whether you're from an underserved community or just curious about space, our materials are designed to be lightweight, interactive, and available in local languages. We also focus on debunking common space myths to provide accurate information.

Myth: Space is Dark

Reality: Space is not always dark. Most often it just needs a medium to reflect light.

Myth: Space is Empty

Reality: Space is not completely empty. It's filled with particles, energy, and celestial bodies.

Myth: Shining Objects are Stars

Reality: Some planets reflect light and appear to shine like stars, but they are not stars.

Myth: All Exoplanets are Like Earth

Reality: Not all exoplanets are Earth-like. Some are called 'Super Earths' because they share similar characteristics, but many are vastly different.
